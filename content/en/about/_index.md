# Hello, I'm Marcílio.

Software Engineer, 34 years old, married and minimalism enthusiast.

**Menos e Mar** is not just a blog, it's a personal experimentation project. The name comes from a play on the Portuguese phrase *"Menos é Mais"* (Less is More), where **MAR** is an extension of myself — **MAR**cílio.

As someone passionate about technology and simplicity, I believe we can explore infinite possibilities with fewer resources, focusing on what truly matters. Here, I share this journey of creation and discovery.
